# ChatGPT Setup

Connect VEED Fabric to ChatGPT to generate AI talking head videos.

## Setup

1. Go to [chatgpt.com](https://chatgpt.com) and log in
2. Open **Settings** -> **Apps**
3. Click **Add more**
4. Enter **VEED Fabric** in the search field and select it
5. Log in with your VEED account when prompted
6. Authorize ChatGPT to access your workspace

## Usage

Once connected, ask ChatGPT to create videos:

- "Create a talking head video introducing my product"
- "How many credits do I have?"
- "Make a video in Spanish"

See [Authentication](./authentication) for more details.
