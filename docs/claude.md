# Claude Setup

Connect VEED Fabric to Claude to generate AI talking head videos.

## Setup

1. Go to [claude.ai](https://claude.ai) and log in
2. Open **Settings** -> **Connectors**
3. Click **Browse connectors**
4. Search for VEED Fabric and select it
5. Click **Connect**
6. Log in with your VEED account when prompted
7. Authorize Claude to access your workspace

## Usage

Once connected, ask Claude to create videos:

- "Create a talking head video introducing my product"
- "How many credits do I have?"
- "Make a video in French"
