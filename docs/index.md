## Overview

VEED Fabric is an MCP server that enables AI assistants to generate professional
talking head videos. Create videos with lifelike AI avatars directly from your
conversation - no camera or video editing skills required.

Turn text into realistic talking head videos featuring lifelike avatars. Perfect
for social media, marketing, or personal content. Create professional videos in
minutes and share them anywhere.

## Features

- **AI Avatar Videos** - 24+ lifelike characters to choose from
- **Multi-Language Voices** - Voices in multiple languages and accents
- **Flexible Aspect Ratios** - Landscape, portrait, or square output
- **Interactive Selection** - Visual widgets for character and voice browsing
- **Real-Time Progress** - Track generation status with live updates
- **Credit Management** - Check balance and see cost estimates before generating
- **Workspace Organization** - Manage videos across multiple workspaces

## Requirements

- **VEED Account** - Sign up at [veed.io](https://www.veed.io)
